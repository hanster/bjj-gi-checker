Description
 
The Fudoshin White Classic BJJ Gi has been designed for the professional Brazilian Jiu Jitsu athlete, looking for a high quality traditional uniform. The Fudoshin Classic BJJ Gi is a high quality uniform made using a traditional 550 GSM gold weave material for the jacket and 14oz twill cotton material for the pants. There is no contrast stitching, no graphical trim and no patches. The only design areas are the Fudoshin logo's embroidered to the arms and the back of the neck.

The Fudoshin Classic BJJ Gi has been reinforced with canvas tape throughout all seams, and the ankle and sleeve cuffs have no fewer than 8 stitching lines, making this Gi one of the highest quality products on the market.
